About me

I am a Software Engineer at Vistaprint in the Boston area.

I hail from Mumbai, India and moved to the United States to pursue my Masters in Computer Science from Northeastern University, Boston, MA.

I'm passionate about building web applications and have been fortunate to learn, grow and pursue this field professionally at Vistaprint - an e-commerce company in the mass customization domain. During my time here, I have been a part of re-building the $1.5B platform from the monolithic architecture to micro-services (ReactJS, Typescript on the front end; Node.js, C# .NET Core, SQL on the back end) and creating internal self-service tools from the ground up to support the newer website.

When not coding, I like to read crime/thriller novels, run long distances, learn a new language (Spanish currently!), go hiking, travel and explore cafes around town.
